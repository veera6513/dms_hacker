MONUMENTS AND STATUES

1.Statue of Unity,gujarat

The Statue of Unity is the world's tallest statue at 182 metres (597 ft), built as an honour to the Iron Man of India, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.

for more info :- Statue of Unity gujarattourism.com

2.Tawang War Memorial, Arunachal Pradesh

Tawang War Memorialis dedicated to the war heroes (soldiers of the Indian Army) of Sino-India war that took place in the year 1962.

Check out the video for reference

3. Descent of the ganges, Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu

Descent of the Ganges is a monument at Mamallapuram, on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal, in the Chengalpattu district of the state of Tamil Nadu, India.

Check out the video for reference

4.Great Buddha Statue, Bodh Gaya, Bihar

The statue is 19.5 m (64 ft) high representing the Buddha seated in a meditation pose, or dhyana mudra, on a lotus in the open air..

Check out the video for reference

5.Statue of Ugra Narsimha, Hampi, Karnataka

Ugra Narasimha Statue in Hampi is a statue of Laxmi Narasimha. The statue is carved out of a single block of stoneThe statue of Ugra Narasimha, Hampi is 6.7 meters in height. The Ugra Narasimha at Hampi is placed on the top of the Hemkunta Hills.

Check out the video for reference

6.Adiyogi Shiva Statue

Surrounded by verdant green farms at the foothills of Velliangiri Mountains in the Western Ghats, Adiyogi Shiva Statue is the world’s biggest bust statue dedicated to the famous Hindu deity Shiva, which is carved out of 500 tonnes of steel.
